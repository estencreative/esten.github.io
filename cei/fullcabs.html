Full Cabs

We provide complete elevator car interiors for all budgets, from first class public facilities and premium office towers to lower rise residential apartment buildings. All models displayed are available in a variety of colors, textures and finishes to suit your individual needs.

A-Look

The A-Look panel system is an ideal solution for cab interior renovations, elevator modernization projects and new cab installations. With an option of several pre-fabricated models and a diverse assortment of colors and finishes to choose from, it’s quick, easy to install and provides excellent value for your elevator interior.

C-Look

The C-Look panel system provides durability and a superior fit and finish for all retro-fit projects and new construction installations. The unique double-sided panel system, which can be installed either vertically or horizontally, allows for damaged wall panels to be replaced simply by reversing the individual panel, saving you time and money while extending the life of your cab interior.

Lightweight Cab

With emphasis on simplicity and great value, Classic’s Light Weight cab is an excellent option to update cab interiors where weight is of significant concern. Materials are applied directly to the cab shell to eliminate extra bulk therefore allowing for quick production and easy installation to meet short project timelines.
