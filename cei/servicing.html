Servicing

We re-clad stainless steel for elevator car & lobby doors, frames, elevator front walls & around button controls. We repair and replace ceilings, handrails, wall panels, glass & mirrors, lighting and flooring. All components are installed with fire-rated materials and specifications to meet elevator code.

Wall Panels

Stainless Steel SS#4, 5WL, D8. A Popular choice for durability alone. On its own, complimented with different finishes or paired with other materials stainless steel provides long lasting, wear resistant elegance that fits most budgets. Perfect for freight elevators or commercial spaces wanting a contemporary industrial feel. Partner stainless steel with mirror or decorative glass for a modern upscale elegance.

Laminates & Veneers Durable, maintenance free, and the most economical option with the largest selection of colors, patterns and texture choices available to fulfill your design needs.

Laminated Mirrors Mirrors are a popular choice for those who want their elevator to seem larger in appearance, or brighter and more welcoming. Mirrors also offer an added sense of personal security for passengers entering elevators in remote or secluded locations. Whether your project objectives are security, maintenance or design driven, mirrors are a popular add to any cab interior.

Lighting & Ceiling Features

Lighting options include traditional incandescent or fluorescent lighting and the highly efficient LED lighting. The LED option consumes a fraction of the power an incandescent light uses to produce the same brightness.

Lighting is available in various ceiling configurations and will be individualized to suit your project needs. Ceiling choices are often influenced by the elevators purpose, height, header placement, hatch access, project design preferences, and may consider the following features:

  1. Marine Lights
  2. Framed or Suspended Fixtures
  3. Valance
  4. Egg crate
  5. Custom/Other

Hand & Bumper Rails

Handrails are available in a flat or round moulding in various sizes and with either a polished or brushed finish. Flat handrails are constructed of solid steel and round handrails are made of hollow stainless-steel tubing, both are also available in brass. Railings are fastened securely through the cab shell from inside the cab for ease of accessibility and safety.

Protective Pads & Pad Hooks

Protective pads are custom made of durable vinyl or cotton exteriors with an interior poly-fil cushion and are fire retardant to meet regulatory specifications. Pads have reinforced buttonholes or grommet eyelets to strengthen hook openings and are individually fitted to protect cab interiors to keep them looking new for longer.

Pad hooks are made of aluminum, stainless steel or brass and are available in various lengths.
